Before You Contact Us

Quick Self-Help:

  • Check if your printer is powered on and connected
  • Verify you have the latest drivers from the manufacturer
  • Try restarting both your printer and computer
  • Ensure your firewall isn't blocking the printer software

What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you more effectively, please provide:

  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Operating system version (Windows 10/11, macOS version)
  • Printer make and model
  • Connection type (USB, Wi-Fi, Ethernet)
  • Any error codes or messages
  • Screenshots (if applicable)

Note: We provide general guidance and troubleshooting tips. For warranty issues or hardware repairs, please contact your printer manufacturer directly.
